About Me

I am Ntare GAMA Allan, a passionate cinematographer, gaffer, director, and entrepreneur committed to storytelling and innovation. Through my work at KCAMEL, I bring cinematic visions to life, mastering lighting design and visual storytelling to create compelling narratives. Beyond film, I am the founder of GAMA TT, an eco-friendly transportation initiative aimed at providing sustainable mobility solutions in Rwanda. My journey blends creativity and entrepreneurship, focusing on projects that inspire change and push boundaries.

When I’m not behind the camera or working on business ideas, I’m deep into anime—from classics to the latest releases, anime fuels my creativity and love for immersive storytelling. I’m also a huge car freak, fascinated by automotive design, speed, and the engineering that pushes machines to their limits. Whether crafting a scene behind the lens, designing impactful solutions for transportation, or geeking out over anime and cars, I strive to tell stories that matter and drive innovation where it's needed most.
